## The Three Types of Superintendents in 2025

**The Resistor** (20% of districts)
- "This AI thing will pass like every other fad"
- Bans ChatGPT, blocks AI tools
- Result: Students use it anyway, teachers feel unsupported, district falls behind

**The Experimenter** (60% of districts)
- "Let's try some pilot programs"
- Allows AI but no clear strategy
- Result: Inconsistent implementation, confused teachers, wasted resources

**The Architect** (20% of districts—where you want to be)
- "AI is our competitive advantage"
- Clear vision, strategic rollout, teacher training, policy framework
- Result: Students prepared for future, teachers empowered, district leads
